語
ON ゴ
KUN かた.る、 かた.らう
English word, speech, language
14 strokes Jōyō group 2 JLPT level N5 Unicode U+8AE9 Popularity: top 25%
Components
一 言 口 五
Notes
Used as suffix for languages: 日本語(にほんご) (Japanese), 英語(えいご) (English), フランス語(ふらんすご) (French) for example.
語る is to talk about, to speak of, to tell a story.
語らう is to talk, to tell, to pledge, to conspire with.
Mnemonic
The radical is 言 (speech), say, word.
It also has the components 五 (five) and 口 (mouth).
So, a language begins when five mouths talk using the same words.

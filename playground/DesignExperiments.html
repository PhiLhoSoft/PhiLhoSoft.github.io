Japanese Kanji Learning

This is a mockup of a page showing a Japanese Kanji character (ideogram of Chinese origin) with its readings (ON = pronunciation of Chinese origin, mostly used in word compounds, KUN = pronunciation of Japanese origin, mostly used in word declinations) and English meaning, the main components of the character, and some additional information and links.

This is mostly to demonstrate I can design a page and implement a design.
It is a kind of work in progress, this one has been made quite quickly (half a day) and could use some more work.
In particular, I have to work on the responsive design, changing the layout for smaller screens.
